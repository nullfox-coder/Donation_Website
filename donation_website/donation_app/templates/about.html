About Us

We are a non-profit
organisation

Sparks Donation is a non-governmental organisation to help children acquire basic needs. We are proudly non-profit, non-corporate, non-compromised. Thousands of people like you help us stand up for better future of younger generations. We rely on donations to carry out our mission to fulfil the dreams of our future generations.

We believe in collaboration as we know this work is bigger than any one organization. We are part of a global network of nonprofit organizations leading the worldwide movement. Will you give today?
